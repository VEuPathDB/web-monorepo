2. Procedure

This document is a guide to predict Cryptosporidium parvum gene.

The first pass annotation relied on simple translation with a 67-amino acid (aa) ORF cutoff. To assist in the identification of compositionally biased unreal ORFs, we constructed a profile of the amino acid composition of all predicted proteins. This analysis identified a large number of ORFs that did not resemble any known proteins or contain any recognizable protein domains. Compositionally biased ORFs of less than 100 aa in length and lacking any recognizable features were not included in the final annotated gene set. The intergenic regions between the remaining ORFs were scanned using translating BLAST searches (GenBank nr) in order to identify small genes and exons that might have been missed.
